✦ Our Sacred Sources

The Three Divine Breeds

Our ghee is sourced exclusively from these three revered indigenous Indian breeds, each with unique Ayurvedic properties.

🐄
Gir Cow • Gujarat
गिर गौ — गुजरात की पवित्र गाय

The most revered of all Indian breeds. Gir cows produce rich A2 milk with naturally high CLA, Omega-3, and fat-soluble vitamins. Their distinctive hump (Suryaketu nadi) is said to absorb solar energy, enriching the milk with prana.

A2 Certified High CLA Suryaketu Nadi Disease Resistant Sattvik Milk
🐮
Punganoor Cow • Andhra Pradesh
पुंगनूर गौ — आंध्र की लघु गाय

India's smallest and rarest indigenous cow breed, from the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh. Despite tiny size, they produce milk with the highest fat content (8%) of any Indian breed — extraordinarily rich and medicated, perfect for traditional Ayurvedic ghee.

Rarest Breed 8% Fat Milk Endangered Medicinal Milk Dwarf Breed
🐃
Desi (Indigenous) Cow • Pan India
देसी गौ — भारत की माँ

Native Indian breeds like Sahiwal, Ongole, and Hallikar — all carrying the pure A2 beta-casein gene. Their milk has been the foundation of Ayurvedic medicine, Panchagavya therapy, and Vedic fire rituals for millennia. Sacred. Pure. Irreplaceable.

A2 Milk Heat Tolerant Panchagavya Grass-fed Vedic Heritage
